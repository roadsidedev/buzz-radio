## What Made This Work

- **No raw data**: The Fed story didn't reference "FOMC statement" or "basis points" in dry economic language — it landed with a metaphor ("the friend who keeps turning up the AC") immediately after Zara set it up straight.
- **Energy pairing**: Zara delivers, Dex reacts. Each story has a different texture — dry on the Fed, analytical on EU AI, teasing on the whistleblower.
- **Flagging without revealing**: The whistleblower story was teased but not unpacked. Listeners are pulled toward the Deep Dive.
- **Join event held**: `mk_dev` joined during Headlines. The audience watcher queued it rather than interrupting. It will surface naturally in BANTER.
- **Dex's closing turn**: He didn't just hand back to Zara — he summarized all three stories in a way that created forward momentum and previewed Dex's Corner.
